WHO WE ARE?

We are a team consists of 6 students have decided to make an application to help the government to reduce the cost of printing metro tickets by changing this method with an application can pass you through the metro stations by NFC technology.
This is will help and facilitate the payment method to many commuters heading to their university or their work standing in queues for too long and spending time to buy a ticket.

ABOUT METROID?

Metroid idea is replaced paper metro tickets and pay through NFC technology, as this technology is available in all mobiles and most devices in the metro, And the application provides you with knowing the time and prices of metro stations, and through the search, you can find out where you are and where you want to go, and all the details of the station will appear to you. And for each user he has a profile and there is a wallet in it, and there is a detail of your old trips and your balance in the wallet. And a payment method through NFC.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our vision is in alignment with Egypt Vision 2030 the transformation of Egypt into a digital society, and Egypt’s digital transformation strategy, that’s why our aim and dream is to reduce Egypt’s paper imports besides the cost of printing metro tickets and to maintain the environment from pollution and replace paper tickets with QR based tickets using smart devices.

Our Mission

We decided to help the government and the environment by replacing paper tickets with electronic tickets through a mobile application using NFC technology, and this is will help and facilitate the payment method to many commuters heading to their university or their work standing in queues for too long and spending time to buy a ticket.

Metroid application is available now on Android and IOS. Download it now!
